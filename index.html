Registry / What to Expect

Delivery & Shipping

Every product is made specially for you after order. We aim for 4–8 days delivery for Metro cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore).

For Northeast India & Remote Regions, delivery typically takes 5–10 days depending on India Post serviceability.

Note: While we aim for speed, delays due to weather or courier logistics are outside our direct control.

Product Expectations

We promise premium fabric and transparent communication. Note that actual colors may slightly vary from mockups due to screen differences and POD fabric absorption.

Returns & Replacements

We support genuine issues: damaged products, printing defects, or wrong items. Please report within 7 days with an unboxing video.

We cannot cover wrong size selection or change of mind as items are printed on demand.

How to choose your Correct Size

Lay a flat t-shirt you already wear. Measure from pit to pit and multiply by 2. Compare with chart above.

Quick Guidance

✔ Medium (M) in most brands → choose M

✔ Large (L) and prefer relaxed fit → choose L or XL

✔ Between sizes → Go one size up for comfort.
